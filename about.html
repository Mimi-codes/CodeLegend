About Us

CodeLegend is a thriving Non-Governmental Organization founded to encourage and empower female developers in various stages of their coding journey in the world of tech.

Our Vision

To provide a flexible access to learning opportunities that can bring a significant change for females in the tech world! Come, and let us show you the beauty of technology!

Our Mission

To mobilize and empower more females in the tech world by providing them with exemplary mentors to help kickstart and walk them through the journey of their chosen career in technology.
